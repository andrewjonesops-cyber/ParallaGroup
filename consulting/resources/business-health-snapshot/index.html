Who This Is For

Business owners and operators who need a clearer view of where the business is performing well, where it is exposed, and where attention is needed before problems compound.

What It Solves

Vague self-assessment, inconsistent operational review, and lack of a simple framework for evaluating overall business condition.

What Is Included

  • Structured assessment across core operating categories
  • Simple 1–5 scoring model
  • Overall business health score
  • Category-level visibility
  • Clear starting point for identifying improvement needs

How It Works

1. Score Each Area

Review each business category and assign a score based on current reality.

2. Review Overall Health

Use the combined score to understand broader business condition.

3. Identify Priorities

Use lower-scoring areas to guide improvement efforts and decision-making.
