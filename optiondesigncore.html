CounterCore™ Program

"We only had one choice" is almost never true.

OptionDesignCore™ helps teams stop pretending there was only one path. It builds a small, realistic set of options for important decisions and makes the tradeoffs explicit — across cost, risk, speed, human impact, and reversibility — so leaders can choose with eyes open. Instead of endless debate or bloated option lists, OptionDesignCore™ delivers a concise, decision-ready set — typically two to four options — plus a documented rationale for the recommended path.

Five Dimensions Every Option Is Evaluated Against

No option is evaluated in isolation. OptionDesignCore™ compares every viable path across the same five dimensions so tradeoffs are visible and defensible.

Cost

Financial, human, and opportunity cost of each path under real constraints.

Risk

What could go wrong, how likely, and what the exposure looks like if it does.

Speed

How quickly each option can be executed relative to what the situation actually requires.

Human Impact

Who is affected, how significantly, and whether the impact is acceptable given stated values.

Reversibility

How easily the decision can be undone or adjusted if conditions change or new information arrives.

What OptionDesignCore™ Does

The goal is faster, clearer, more defensible decisions — not an exhaustive catalog of every conceivable path.

Option Generation & Refinement

Structuring the work of identifying and refining viable options so teams move beyond default paths and hidden assumptions — without drifting into analysis paralysis or endless brainstorming.

False Binary Elimination

Identifying when a decision has been framed as a choice between only two paths — and surfacing the alternatives that exist but haven't been articulated, often because the default felt inevitable.

Tradeoff Mapping

Making the real tradeoffs of each option explicit and comparable — across cost, risk, speed, human impact, and reversibility — so the choice is made with full visibility rather than incomplete information.

Decision-Ready Short List

Every OptionDesignCore™ engagement ends with a concise set — typically two to four options — that are grounded in real constraints and ready for a decision. Not a catalog. A short list with a recommended path.

Documented Recommendation Rationale

The recommended option includes documented reasoning — why this path, what tradeoffs were accepted, and why other options were set aside. Decisions become defensible and auditable.

Time-Boxed to Decision Urgency

The depth of option design is calibrated to the importance and urgency of the decision — major strategic decisions warrant more rigor than tactical calls. OptionDesignCore™ is not a process applied uniformly regardless of stakes.

On false binaries and momentum: Most decisions that get framed as binary — "we can either do X or do nothing" — are not actually binary. The binary framing usually reflects momentum, politics, or the limits of what has been considered rather than what actually exists. OptionDesignCore™ exists to break that frame before resources are committed to a path that was never fully chosen.

How We Work
Grounded in Real Constraints

Options are generated within actual budget, timeline, legal, and operational constraints — not theoretical ideals. An option that cannot be executed is not an option.

Concise by Design

More options is not always better. OptionDesignCore™ targets the smallest set of meaningfully different paths — typically two to four — that covers the real decision space without overwhelming it.

Ends With a Recommendation

The output always includes a recommended path alongside the alternatives — with documented reasoning. The goal is a decision, not an options menu that defers accountability.

Accountability Preserved

OptionDesignCore™ structures and clarifies the decision — it does not make it. Authority and accountability for the final choice remain with the appropriate decision-maker.

What OptionDesignCore™ Is Not

These guardrails keep the program practical, bounded, and firmly oriented toward actionable decisions rather than endless deliberation.

✕

An excuse for analysis paralysis

✕

A full business case engine for every conceivable alternative

✕

A debate club with no resolution

✕

A "more options is always better" exercise

✕

A mechanism to delay or diffuse accountability for making a choice

All OptionDesignCore™ work must be time-boxed relative to the importance and urgency of the decision; produce a clear recommendation alongside alternatives; document key tradeoffs and constraints; and avoid being used to delay or diffuse accountability for making a choice.

These guardrails keep the program practical, bounded, and firmly oriented toward actionable decisions — not endless deliberation or responsibility avoidance.

If your organization is stuck in a false binary, defaulting to the path of least resistance, or making decisions that can't be explained after the fact — OptionDesignCore™ is built to change that.
